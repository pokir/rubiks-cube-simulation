Rubik's Cube Simulation

Basic moves: L, R, U, D, F, B

Rotate the cube: x, y, z

Add a w after the move to turn two layers at once (for example: Rw, Dw')

Add a 2 after the move to turn the layer twice (for example: R2, Lw2, y2)
